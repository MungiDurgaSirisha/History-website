KUNDAN BAGH

A discussion on some strange events or stories always lead to such arguments. It may be thrilling for a few and uninteresting for many but we can't stop talking about the spine-chilling mystery. The story of Kundanbagh Haunted House in Hyderabad created quite a sensation in the year 2002. Even to this day, it is considered as one of the top haunted places in the city of Nizams - Hyderabad.

MYSTERY

A family of 4 (a mother, father and two daughters) lived in thehouse of Kundanbagh colony. It is told that, one day the father left the house and never returned. Nobody knows anything about him ever since then. According to some neighbours, the mother and the daughters were too weird. The daughters used to play outside the house with a blood filled bottle. . The neighbours also used to hear some weird sounds from inside the house.
