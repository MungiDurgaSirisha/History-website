VALMIKI'S RAMAYANA

BALAKANDA

The origins and childhood of Rama. Sita’s birth, betrothal, and marriage to Rama.

AYODHYAKANDA

The preparations for Rama’s coronation in the city of Ayodhya, his exile into the forest, and the regency of Bharata.

ARANYAKANDA

The forest exile of Rama with Sita and Lakshmana. The kidnapping of Sita by the demon king Ravana.

KISKINDAKANDA

Rama and Hanuman in Kishkindha.

SUNDARAKANDA

Detailed account of Hanuman’s adventures, including his meeting with Sita.

YUDDHAKANDA

The battle in Lanka between Rama and Ravana. Sita’s fire ordeal. Rama’s return to Ayodhya to reign over the ideal state.

UTTARAKANDA

Sita’s banishment. Lava and Kusha. Rama’s dharma fulfilled.
